Q: Let's say you're standing on the top left of a grid of dimensions nxm.
From every cell you can either move right or move down or move diagonally in the
downright direction. Calculate total ways to go from top left to bottom right.

(i, j) -> (i+1, j), (i, j+1), (i+1, j+1)
